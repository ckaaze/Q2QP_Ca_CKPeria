Initiatives

Educational Outreach Programs:

  • Conduct workshops and seminars in schools and communities to educate people about responsible pet ownership, animal behavior, and the importance of spaying/neutering.
  • Develop educational materials like brochures and pamphlets to distribute in schools, veterinary clinics, and community centers.

    • Animal Rescue and Rehabilitation:
  • Strengthen partnerships with local rescue groups and establish a network for the rescue, rehabilitation, and rehoming of abused or neglected animals.
  • Seek support from the community through volunteer programs and donations.

    • Animal Adoption Drives:
  • Host regular adoption events to help find loving homes for abandoned or stray animals.
  • Promote adoption through social media, local newspapers, and community bulletin boards.

    • Involvements

    Volunteer Programs:

  • Establish volunteer programs for individuals to contribute their time and skills to various PAWS initiatives, such as animal rescue, education programs, fundraising events, and administrative tasks.

    • Youth Ambassadors:
  • Engage young advocates by forming a youth ambassador program. These ambassadors can lead school clubs, organize awareness campaigns, and participate in educational outreach activities.

    • Pet Adoption Advocacy:
  • Develop a network of advocates who actively promote pet adoption. These advocates can share success stories, organize adoption events, and encourage responsible pet ownership.

    • Educational Outreach Teams:
  • Form teams of volunteers to conduct educational outreach programs in schools, communities, and events. These teams can deliver presentations, distribute educational materials, and answer questions about animal welfare.

    • Activities and Participation

    PAWS ANIMAL REHABILITATION CENTER (PARC)

  • PAWS also runs an animal shelter for over 300 cats and dogs rescued from cruelty or neglect. Here, these animals are rehabilitated in the hope of finding them new homes and a second chance at a good life. There is also a low-cost clinic that provides veterinary services to the indigent community, including spay and neuter (kapon).

    • Pet Care Webinars and Q&A Sessions:
  • Host live webinars with veterinarians or animal behavior experts to discuss common pet care issues. Allow users to submit questions in advance or during the session for a Q&A segment.

    • Pet of the Month Showcase:
  • Feature a "Pet of the Month" on the website, sharing stories and photos of rescued animals. Users can nominate their pets, and winning entries could receive special recognition.

    • Virtual Pet Adoption Events:
  • Organize virtual pet adoption events where users can explore profiles of animals in need of homes, interact with them through videos or live streams, and initiate the adoption process through the website.
